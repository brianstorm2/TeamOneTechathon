What is Investing?

Investing is the process of putting your money into financial assets with the expectation of generating a profit over time. In the UK, there are numerous investment opportunities, ranging from traditional savings accounts to more complex financial instruments like stocks, bonds, and real estate. Investing allows your money to grow and helps you achieve financial goals such as retirement, property ownership, or wealth building.

  • Beat Inflation: Investments typically offer higher returns than savings accounts, helping your money retain its value over time.

  • Diversify Income: Investments can provide additional income streams.
  • Financial Security: Investing prepares you for major life events and retirement.
