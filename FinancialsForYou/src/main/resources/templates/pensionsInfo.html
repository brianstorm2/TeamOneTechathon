Understanding Pensions

Pensions are retirement savings plans designed to provide income when you stop working. They grow over time through contributions and investment returns.

Types of Pensions in the UK

  • State Pension: Provided by the government.
  • Workplace Pension: Set up by your employer.
  • Personal Pension: Set up by yourself.
