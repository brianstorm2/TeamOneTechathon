The Problem: Financial Education in the UK

Financial literacy in the UK is at a critical juncture. Many adults lack the essential knowledge to manage their finances effectively, resulting in debt accumulation, poor savings habits, and financial instability. According to the Financial Conduct Authority (FCA), 24% of UK adults have low financial resilience, leaving them vulnerable to financial shocks.

Despite initiatives like the UK Strategy for Financial Wellbeing, financial education remains limited, especially regarding key areas such as mortgages, pensions, savings, and investments. Research by the Organisation for Economic Co-operation and Development (OECD) shows that only 38% of UK adults understand basic financial concepts such as interest rates and inflation.

Our Mission

At Financials4U, our mission is to bridge the financial literacy gap by providing accessible, user-friendly tools and educational resources. We empower individuals with the knowledge they need to make informed decisions and build sustainable financial futures.
