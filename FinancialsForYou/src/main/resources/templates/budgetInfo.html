What is Budgeting?

Budgeting helps you plan and control your income and expenses. It's the key to financial freedom and achieving your goals.

🚀 Steps to Create a Budget

  1. 📊 Calculate Your Income: Know how much money you bring in monthly.
  2. 📝 Track Your Spending: Understand where your money goes.
  3. 🎯 Set Clear Financial Goals: Define what you’re saving for.
  4. 💳 Allocate Funds Wisely: Apply the 50/30/20 rule or similar.
  5. 🔄 Review Regularly: Adjust your budget as needed.

Frequently Asked Questions

📊 How much should I save each month?

Financial experts recommend saving at least 20% of your income.

💳 How do I reduce debt while saving?

Prioritise high-interest debts and create a debt repayment plan while saving small amounts.
